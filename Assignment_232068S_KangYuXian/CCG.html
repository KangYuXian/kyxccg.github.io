INTRO-DESCRIPTION

Hi, We are a Performing Arts CCA in NYP that enriches in the chinese cultural and provides more opportunities for students to hone their performing arts talent/skills.

We welcome any NYP students interested in the chinese cultural.

We have 4 Sub-Groups for you to choose from

  1. Vocal
  2. Dance
  3. Calligraphy
  4. Drama

Do not worry if you do no have any background/experience in any of these areas. We have wonderful and experienced seniors and teachers to help you improve

There will not be any audition!!

Come Join our CCA! Lets make your time in poly a more memorable part of your life!!
